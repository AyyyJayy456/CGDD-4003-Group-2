Group 2 Version: 

Using Unity to recreate Pong provides a 2D physics-based simulation environment. Using Unity provides a flat, bounded game environment driven by its 2D physics engine. 

We made our game a little bit more vibrant in terms of colors, our background is blue and when entering the 2 player modes the paddles are colored to represent the players which the OG pong did not do. Also adding in some music to keep the original feel of the game and sounds, we should have added in a scoring sound but overall the gameplay is straightforward, Steven even got some people to test it and they said it was fun. Instead of having one mode we have three! This was to make the game more playable for a single user experience but also adding upon the idea of the OG which was to play with people! We have the paddles also move 360 degrees around in a circle instead of just up and down, engaging more of the screen and making the user to think more about which direction to move in order to efficiently hit the ball in the direction that its headed.


How to Play 

SINGLE PLAYER: A or D on the keyboard or the LEFT ARROW KEY or RIGHT ARROW KEY
Goal: Hit the ball for as long as possible!

VERSUS: LEFT ARROW and RIGHT ARROW for 1st player and A and D for 2nd player!
goal: First to score to 11 is the winner! Scoring occurs when its knocked to the opposite side of the players area

CO-OP: LEFT ARROW and RIGHT ARROW for 1st player and A and D for 2nd player!
goal: Hit the ball for as long as possible and work together!
